Bulldog

Size: Medium

Color: Various (Most commonly brindle, white, and fawn)

Temperament: Docile, Willful, Friendly

Description: The Bulldog is a medium-sized breed known for its muscular build, loose skin, and distinctive pushed-in nose. Despite their tough appearance, Bulldogs are affectionate, gentle, and good with children, making them great family pets.
